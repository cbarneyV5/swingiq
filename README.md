# SwingIQ — Professional Golf Swing Analyzer

Upload your golf swing video and get a full coaching breakdown powered by computer vision.

**What it does:**
- Tracks 33 body landmarks across every frame of your swing
- Scores you on 8 biomechanical metrics including X-Factor, hip rotation, tempo, and spine stability
- Overlays a pro reference skeleton on your video so you can see the exact differences
- Gives you specific, named drills to fix every fault
- Generates a full interactive coaching report

**How to use it:**
1. Optionally upload a pro reference video first
2. Upload your swing video
3. Click Analyze — processing takes 2 to 5 minutes
4. Watch your annotated video and review your coaching report

**Tips for best results:**
- Film from down the line or face on
- Keep your full body visible from head to feet
- Good lighting makes a big difference
- Keep videos under 60 seconds for faster processing
